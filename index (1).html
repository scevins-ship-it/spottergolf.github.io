// Technology

THE TECH
INSIDE

First-to-market combination of AI, LiDAR and AR for golf ball detection. Not possible until now.

Computer Vision / YOLOv11

Custom-trained object detection model using bounding box detection and confidence scoring. 97.2% mAP accuracy across varied environmental conditions.

LiDAR Depth Sensing

Captures millions of precise distance data points per second. Measures exact ball distance and projects 3D world position for AR guidance.

Augmented Reality Trail

AR overlay guides the golfer from their position to the ball's exact 3D location. Built on ARKit with ARWorldTracking configuration.

On-Device Processing

All AI inference runs on the device using Core ML. No data transmitted. No latency. No dependency on network connectivity.

PRIVACY
POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Spotter Golf Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Spotter Golf mobile application ("the App") and website at spottergolf.com.

1. Who We Are

Spotter Golf Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. You can contact us at contact@spottergolf.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account information: Name and email address provided via Sign in with Apple, if you choose to create an account.
  • Profile information: Country, home golf club, handicap index, and ball markings that you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage data: Rounds played, balls found, scans performed, courses visited, and app interaction data.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates at the time of ball detection, used to log find locations and support course check-in features. Location is only accessed while you are actively using the App.
  • Device information: Device type, iOS version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's core ball-detection features
  • To personalise your experience and display your stats and history
  • To send monthly usage summaries via local notifications (if you grant permission)
  • To improve our AI detection model using anonymised, aggregated detection data
  • To communicate with you about your account or subscription
  • To comply with our legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the App services you have requested.
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our AI model and app features using anonymised usage data.
  • Consent: For optional features such as location tracking, advertising personalisation, and notifications. You may withdraw consent at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Apple: For Sign in with Apple authentication and App Store payment processing.
  • Analytics providers: Anonymised, aggregated usage data only.
  • Legal authorities: Where required by law.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your personal data is stored securely. Usage stats and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. Where data is transmitted to our servers, it is encrypted in transit using TLS. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, use the "Delete Account & Data" option within the App, or contact us at contact@spottergolf.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. App Tracking Transparency

If you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, we may use your data for personalised advertising and analytics. You can change this permission at any time in your iPhone Settings.

10. Location Data

Location access is used solely to log the GPS coordinates of golf ball finds and to identify nearby courses. We request "While Using the App" location permission only. Location data is not shared with third parties and is stored locally on your device.

11. Cameras and Sensors

The App uses your iPhone camera and LiDAR sensor to scan for golf balls. The camera feed is processed entirely on-device in real time. No camera footage or images are captured, stored, or transmitted.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the App. The current version is always available at spottergolf.com/privacy.

13. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:
contact@spottergolf.com
